About Us

Charlie’s Café, one of sun devil dining’s local restaurant partners, brings the taste of Europe to ASU. Proudly serving Lavazza Italian espresso and featuring fresh European sandwiches and baked goods. Come experience the difference! With three locations on campus, there is always a Charlie’s Café near! Charlie’s Café is located at the Hayden Library, BioDesign Building and the College of Design.
